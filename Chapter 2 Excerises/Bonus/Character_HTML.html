Characters

  • Harry Potter: The protagonist and "The Boy Who Lived," known for his lightning-shaped scar and courage.
  • Hermione Granger: Harry's intelligent and resourceful best friend.
  • Ron Weasley: Harry's loyal and humorous best friend.
  • Severus Snape: The complex and enigmatic Potions Master at Hogwarts with a mysterious past.
